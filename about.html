About OriRank

OriRank is a fan-made tracker for Oricon weekly manga and light novel sales in Japan. We provide up-to-date rankings with clean visuals, hover details, and direct Amazon links to support creators.

This site is for informational and entertainment purposes only. All sales data is sourced from Oricon public releases.
